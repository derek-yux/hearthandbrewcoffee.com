The Science of the Perfect Maple Latte

Creating an exceptional maple latte requires understanding both coffee chemistry and the unique properties of real maple syrup. Unlike artificial flavors or simple syrups, authentic maple syrup contains over 300 flavor compounds that interact with coffee's complex profile.

Our baristas undergo specialized training in maple latte preparation, including:

  • Espresso extraction timing adjusted to 28-30 seconds for optimal balance with maple sweetness
  • Milk selection guidance helping customers choose between whole, oat, or almond based on desired richness
  • Maple integration technique stirring maple into espresso before adding milk for better distribution
  • Seasonal adjustments accounting for maple syrup's viscosity changes with temperature
  • Consistency protocols ensuring every maple latte meets our quality standards
