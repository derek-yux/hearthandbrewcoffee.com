Coffee & Espresso

Maple Latte $5.25

Espresso with steamed milk and authentic Grade A Canadian maple syrup – a true taste of home.

Fireside Mocha $5.75

Rich espresso with dark chocolate, a whisper of cinnamon, and a hint of cayenne for a comforting warmth.

Cold Brew $4.50

Steeped for 18 hours for an incredibly smooth, rich, and low-acidity coffee experience.
